Upgrading of Territorial Scope

Requirements:

  1. Accomplished Other BN-Related Application Form, and Application Form signed by the owner
  2. Valid government-issued ID, for verification
  3. Original Business Name Certificate (if available)

Call your DTI Regional or Provincial Office first and inquire the nearest DTI Office or Negosyo Center where you can avail the service. Click the link to access the DTI Directory of Key Officials: https://www.dti.gov.ph/contact/.
