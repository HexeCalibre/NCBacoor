Cancellation

Yes, you can apply for a cancellation of BN registration at selected DTI Office* for any of the following reasons:
  1. Cessation of business operations prior to the expiry date of the existing registration;
  2. Sale or transfer of the business to any other person;
  3. Upon BN owner's conclusive determination that a prior owner and lawful user of an identical or confusingly similar BN exists;
  4. Upon transfer of the business to another geographical location beyond or outside the registered BN's territorial scope.

Requirements:

  1. Accomplished Other BN-Related Application Form, signed by the owner
  2. Valid government-issued ID, for verification
  3. Affidavit of Cancellation under oath stating: that no financial obligation subsists and that the cancellation is not intended to defraud creditors.
  4. Original Business Name Certificate (if available only)
  5. Authorization letter, if filed by a representative and representative's valid ID

Call your DTI Regional or Provincial Office first and inquire the nearest DTI Office or Negosyo Center where you can avail the service. Click the link to access the DTI Directory of Key Officials: https://www.dti.gov.ph/contact/.
